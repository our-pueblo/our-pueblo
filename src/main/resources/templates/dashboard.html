Welcome and thank you for registering to use Our Pueblo! Your efforts as a volunteer are essential to this programs existence.


If this is your first time using the site here are some tips to get you started!

  • Use the side nav to get around the site, just click the icon in the top left ot open it.
  • New Translation will give you a list of requests for translations we have, click on one and you will see the original page text along with a Google translation of the page, make your edits to the translation then just hit submit, an admin will review it as soon as they can!
  • Your Translations will show all the translations you have made, and if they have yet to be approved bvy an admin it will let you edit or delete them.
  • Your Profile is just that, your profile! Here you can see what contact details you gave us and change them if you need to
